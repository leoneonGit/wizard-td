🌿 Wildguard TD — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

The short version

Wildguard TD collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no data is ever sent to us or anyone else.

What the game stores

Your game progress (saved runs, unlocks, and settings) is stored only on your own device using your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device. Deleting the app or clearing site data erases it.

Network use

The game downloads its art and code from our server when you install or update it, and can then be played offline. No information about you or your play is uploaded.

Children

The game is suitable for general audiences and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes & contact

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page. Questions? Contact the developer via the project page.
